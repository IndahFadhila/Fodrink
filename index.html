Simple Egg Ceplok Stews Recipe for Sahur Side Dishes

To save money and still have a delicious Suhoor meal this Ramadan 2024, try this egg stew recipe! Chicken eggs are a great source of protein that keeps you full for a long time. So it's perfect for those of you who want to find an economical menu for sahur. A durable side dish is often used as an option for fasting month dishes. The reason is, this dish can be reheated and the taste does not change. Translated with DeepL.com (free version)

Stews are one of the most popular dishes during Ramadan. You can customize this ceplok egg stew recipe so that it is still delicious to serve for the next day's suhoor. Make the stew seasoning and cook it the day before. Then store it in the refrigerator. Towards dawn, you can simply make ceplok eggs, then warm the stew sauce. Add this sunny side up egg, and cook over low heat until the stew seasoning is absorbed. For the secret to delicious stews, don't forget to use Bango Kecap Manis. The blend of sweet soy sauce from quality black soybeans, makes the taste of traditional dishes maximum delicious.

Egg Stew Ingredients and Seasoning

  • 8 chicken eggs, make sunny side up eggs
  • 4 tbsp Bango Kecap Manis
  • 1 tsp Royco Chicken Broth
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 250 ml water
  • 2 tbsp oil, for sautéing

Fine Seasoning:

  • 7 shallots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 candlenuts
  • 2 red chili

How to make

  1. Heat oil, saute spices together with bay leaves and ground nutmeg until fragrant.
  2. Pour water, cook until boiling.
  3. Add Bango Kecap Manis and Royco Chicken Broth, stir well. Cook until absorbed.
  4. Add the sunny side up egg. Reduce heat, cook until absorbed. Remove and serve.

Soft and Delicious Chocolate Milkshake

Indulge in the creamy richness of our Soft and Delicious Chocolate Milkshake! Every sip offers a smooth, velvety blend of rich chocolate flavor and silky milk, perfectly chilled to satisfy your sweet cravings. Whether you're enjoying it as a decadent treat or a refreshing dessert, this milkshake is a chocolate lover's dream come true. Simple to make yet irresistibly indulgent, it's the ultimate comfort drink that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 50g chocolate ice cream, for thickening
  • 5 tbsp chocolate syrup (can also use powdered chocolate)
  • 250 ml sweet chocolate milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence, ready to use

How To Make:

  1. Combine and blend all ingredients using a blender for ease.
  2. First, pour the sweet chocolate milk into the blender.
  3. Next, add the chocolate ice cream, chocolate syrup, and a little vanilla essence to enhance the flavor.
  4. Turn on the blender at high speed until all ingredients are well-mixed and thickened. Wait for just a few minutes.
  5. Finally, serve the chocolate milkshake in a prepared glass or cup.

The chocolate milkshake is now ready to be enjoyed!
